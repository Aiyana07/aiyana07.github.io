ABOUT US

Feed Someone Today is a charity organization that has been committed to ending world hunger for nearly 20 years, spanning almost 50 countries. The organization saves the lives of children and their families through detection (especially after disaster strikes or war), prevention - including teaching communities how they can be independent providers themselves - all while working until there's finally no more need or desire within our ever-growing global population! Almost all of the money donated — about 95 cents of every dollar — goes toward programs to help cure the world hunger problem.

Feed Someone Today was founded in 2003 with a team on a mission to end life-threatening hunger worldwide. They have been treating and preventing malnutrition across more than 45 countries, working with children in particular who are often victims of circumstance or conflict zones where they cannot always afford adequate food intake due their poverty level while also ensuring mothers do not go hungry during pregnancy because it’s unacceptable within our society today as well when you consider all those pregnant ladies out there just waiting for something bad happen so that we can help them again!

Our Humble Beginning

The Founding members of the foundation set apart to tour West Africa after the first official funding event held on September, 2003 in California, USA. To achieve this, they raised about $85,000 via donations and a state wide fundraising campaign tagged "Operation Pacify World Hunger". Ivory Coast was the major country of focus due to the First Ivorian Civil War that spanned from September 19, 2002 – March 4, 2007. Lots of relief materials like Bags of rice, cereals, legumes,toiletries, livestocks, clothing materials, hospitalization and even housing. The West Africa, 2003 tour lasted for about nine weeks and was a resounding success. A 2004 Charity tour was spreaded out to other neighbouring West African countries like Senegal, Nigeria, Ghana and Benin Republic. By 2022, Feed Someone Today Charity Foundation have had an impact in over 45+ countries across Africa, Latin America and East Asia. With Increased funding from generous donors like You, we aspire to cover every nation of the earth via our reliable partners and ever resilient team.
