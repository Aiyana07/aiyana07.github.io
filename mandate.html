Financial Breakdown

By operating efficiently with minimal fundraising and administrative costs; Feed Someone Today Charity Foundation is able to commit $0.95 of every dollar directly into field programs. We work hard to ensure that our programs are efficient and effective. We have a rigorous assessment process for independently evaluating the impact, coverage, coherence with other priorities in local communities where we operate; external audits allow us as an organization make sure everything is on track from time-to-time.
We are committed to a policy of transparency and disclosure by ensuring that key financial information is publicly available with guarantees of good management.

Our Mission Statement


    1. Lifesaving impact in 45+ countries.
    2. 500+ field staff assisting more than 2.5 million people.
    3. 20 years of expertise responding to emergencies caused by conflict, natural disasters, and food crises.
Our global efforts save thousands of lives each year, but millions of undernourished children remain in need of lifesaving treatment. We are committed to helping all children access the urgent hunger care they need to survive. The world needs a better way to deal with hunger. Together, by partnering us via your generous donations, we can dream to achieve a world where no child goes hungry for a day!

Our Vision


    There are over 811 million people world wide, go to bed hungry every night; and one third of all children in low-income countries suffers from chronic malnutrition. Nearly half of all child deaths worldwide are from causes related to undernutrition, even though it is:
    PREDICTABLE: We know where and when to expect acute undernutrition.
    PREVENTABLE: We know how to help children stay healthy.
    TREATABLE: Community-based outpatient programs and therapeutic ready-to-use foods can help save millions, and just $50 can provide a child with lifesaving treatment.
